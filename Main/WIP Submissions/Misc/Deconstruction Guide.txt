[head=2][color=MidnightBlue]Deconstruction Procedures[/color][/head]
▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄▄

[head=3][bullet/][color=MidnightBlue]Table Deconstruction[/color][/head]
1.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]wrench [/color][/bold]on the table to unfasten it.
2.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]wrench [/color][/bold]again on the table parts to fully dismantle them.

[head=3][bullet/][color=MidnightBlue]Rack Deconstruction[/color][/head]
1.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]wrench [/color][/bold]and ensure it is held in your active hand.
2.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]wrench [/color][/bold]again on the table parts to fully dismantle them.

[head=3][bullet/][color=MidnightBlue]Handrail Deconstruction[/color][/head]
1.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]wrench [/color][/bold]to unfasten the handrail.
2.Follow up with a [bold][color=DarkRed]screwdriver[/color][/bold] to fully deconstruct it into metal.

[head=3][bullet/][color=MidnightBlue]Sandbag Deconstruction[/color][/head]
1.Hold an [bold][color=DarkRed]entrenching tool.[/color][/bold]
2.Use it on the sandbag wall to break it down.

[head=3][bullet/][color=MidnightBlue]Window (and Frame) Deconstruction[/color][/head]
[bullet/]If you want to keep the window [color=MidnightBlue]intact for relocation[/color]:
1.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]screwdriver[/color][/bold] to remove the paneling.
2.Use a crowbar to detach the glass.
3.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]wrench [/color][/bold]on the window frame to remove it.

[bullet/]If you want to [color=FireBrick]destroy the window[/color] for materials:
1.Bash the reinforced glass window using any object
2.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]wrench [/color][/bold]on the window frame to remove it.

[head=3][bullet/][color=MidnightBlue]Chair Deconstruction[/color][/head]
1.Use a [bold][color=DarkRed]screwdriver[/color][/bold] on the chair.

[head=3][bullet/][color=MidnightBlue]Cade Deconstruction[/color][/head]
[bold][color=DarkRed]Wirecutters[/color] [/bold]can be used to remove the [color=DarkRed]Barbed Wire[/color].

1.Use the [bold][color=DarkRed]screwdriver[/color][/bold] on the Barricade.
2.Use the [bold][color=DarkRed]wrench [/color][/bold]on the Barricade.
([italic]just make sure it's unanchored[/italic]).
3.Use the [bold][color=DarkRed]crowbar [/color][/bold]on the Barricade.
